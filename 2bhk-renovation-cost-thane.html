2BHK Renovation Cost in Thane (2026 Guide)

SPD Renovation Team April 18, 2026 8 min read Thane, Maharashtra

If you own a 2BHK flat in Thane and are thinking about giving it a fresh look, one of the first questions that comes to mind is: how much will it cost? With rising material prices and a booming construction market across Thane West, having an accurate 2026 cost estimate before you begin is essential.

In this comprehensive guide, the SPD Renovation team breaks down every major expense category for a typical 600–800 sq ft 2BHK flat in Thane — from civil work and plumbing to interior design and waterproofing — so you can plan your budget with confidence.

📌 Quick Takeaway: A full renovation of a 2BHK in Thane typically costs between ₹4.5 Lakhs to ₹12 Lakhs in 2026, depending on the scope, finish level (basic, standard, or premium), and your choice of materials and brands.

Factors That Affect Renovation Costs in Thane

Before diving into the numbers, it's important to understand what drives renovation costs in Thane. The city has a diverse property market — from affordable societies in Kalwa and Mumbra to ultra-premium towers in Hiranandani Estate and Ghodbunder Road. Renovation costs vary accordingly.

  • Flat Size: Most 2BHKs in Thane range from 550 sq ft (RERA carpet area) to 900 sq ft (built-up). Larger units cost more to renovate.
  • Age of the Property: Older buildings (10+ years) typically need more structural and plumbing work than newer ones.
  • Scope of Work: A cosmetic refresh (paint + flooring) costs far less than a full gut renovation (demolition + civil rebuild + new interiors).
  • Material Grade: Using premium brands like Asian Paints Royale, Jaquar fittings, or Hafele hardware significantly increases the budget.
  • Labour Rates in Thane: Skilled contractor rates in Thane West range from ₹400–₹800/day per worker in 2026.

Civil Work Costs in a 2BHK Thane Flat

Civil work forms the backbone of any renovation. In Thane, this typically includes:

  • Demolition of old tiles, walls, or partitions
  • Masonry work — brickwork for new walls or room extensions
  • Plastering of walls and ceilings
  • Beam-filling and crack repairs for older buildings
  • Bathroom civil: waterproof PCC bed, tile bedding

Civil Work Cost Per Sq Ft in Thane (2026)

Basic plastering: ₹35–₹55/sq ft. Wall tile fixing (including material): ₹80–₹160/sq ft. Full room partition in AAC blocks: ₹120–₹200/sq ft.

Electrical Work Costs for 2BHK

Rewiring a 2BHK is essential if the flat is more than 10–12 years old. Poor wiring is a fire hazard, and upgrading to modern MCB panels and ISI-marked cables (like Polycab 4mm FR wires) is worth every rupee.

What Does Electrical Work Include?

  • Replacing all internal wiring (PVC conduit)
  • New MCB/ELCB distribution board
  • Installing switches, sockets, fans, exhaust, geysers
  • Smart home automation (in premium packages)
  • Ambient & task lighting for living, kitchen, and bedrooms

SPD uses Polycab wires and Havells / Legrand modular switches — quality that lasts 20+ years without issues.

Plumbing Renovation Costs

Plumbing is one of the most underrated renovation costs. In Thane's older societies, the internal piping is often galvanized iron (GI) which corrodes over time. Replacing it with CPVC or UPVC pipes significantly reduces leakage problems.

Plumbing Work Scope in a 2BHK

  • Replacement of all internal water supply lines (hot & cold)
  • New drainage pipes (SWR/PVC)
  • 2 bathroom fittings: EWC, basin, tap, shower, geyser point
  • Kitchen sink, mixer tap, water purifier point
  • Bathroom waterproofing before tile-laying

Using Jaquar or Kohler fittings can add ₹40,000–₹80,000 to the plumbing budget but delivers a luxury feel and multi-year warranty.

Interior Design & Modular Kitchen

The most significant cost impact comes from interior design — especially modular kitchens and wardrobes. In Thane, modular furniture prices have risen about 12–15% compared to 2024 due to higher plywood and hardware costs.

Modular Kitchen Cost in Thane (2026)

  • Flat-panel shutters (basic): ₹65,000 – ₹90,000 for a 10ft kitchen
  • Profile handles (standard): ₹1,00,000 – ₹1,60,000
  • Acrylic / PU finish (premium): ₹1,80,000 – ₹3,50,000

Wardrobe Cost for 2 Bedrooms

A sliding-door wardrobe for 2 bedrooms (8–10 ft each) costs approximately ₹60,000–₹1,20,000 in standard laminate finish. Premium options with soft-close channels and profile lights can go up to ₹2,00,000.

7 Smart Tips to Save on Your 2BHK Renovation in Thane

Get Multiple Quotes

Compare at least 2–3 contractors before finalising. Prices vary significantly even in the same locality.

Renovate Off-Season

Avoid Oct–Dec when demand peaks. Jan–March often has better labour availability and lower rates.

Buy Materials Yourself

Purchase tiles, fittings, and switches directly from shops like Supreme, Jain & Sons in Thane to save contractor markup.

Refurbish vs Replace

Reskin existing kitchen cabinet doors with laminates instead of full replacement — saves ₹30,000–₹50,000.

Fix-Price Contract

Always get a written scope of work with fixed prices to avoid cost overruns midway through the project.

Bundle Work Together

Getting civil + electrical + plumbing done by one contractor like SPD saves 10–15% compared to hiring separate teams.

Prioritise Critical Repairs

Fix waterproofing, wiring, and drainage first. Cosmetic work can always be done in phases later.

Negotiate Labour + Material

Ask for bulk discounts if you're doing full flat renovation — many suppliers in Thane offer 5–10% off for volume orders.

What is the average cost to fully renovate a 2BHK in Thane in 2026?

A full renovation of a standard 2BHK (650–750 sq ft) in Thane costs between ₹5.5 Lakhs to ₹9 Lakhs for standard quality work, and up to ₹12–16 Lakhs for premium finishes. Basic renovations (paint, flooring, minor plumbing) can be done in ₹3.5–5 Lakhs.

Can I renovate my 2BHK in stages to manage the budget?

Absolutely. Many homeowners start with the most critical work — civil repairs, waterproofing, and electrical — and then do cosmetic upgrades (interiors, painting, modular furniture) in subsequent phases. SPD can plan a phased project to suit your budget and timeline.

What is included in a "full renovation" package?

A full renovation typically covers: demolition of existing tiles and fixtures, civil masonry, complete rewiring, new plumbing pipes and fittings, bathroom and kitchen waterproofing, new flooring, painting, modular kitchen, wardrobes, false ceiling, and lighting. How do material costs in Thane compare to Mumbai?

Material costs in Thane are roughly 5–12% lower than in Mumbai (South Bombay), partly due to lower transport costs and better warehouse availability on the Eastern & Western Expressways. Labour rates are also marginally more competitive, making Thane an excellent value for quality renovation.
