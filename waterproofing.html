Protection Tips

Waterproofing FAQs

Don't wait for the leak. Get expert answers on how to protect your investment.

Can you fix dampness from the inside?

Internal dampness is usually a symptom. While we can use crystalline coatings for negative-side pressure, we always recommend identifying and fixing the external source for a permanent solution.

What is the best time for terrace waterproofing?

The pre-monsoon window (March-May) is ideal as the surfaces are bone-dry, allowing for maximum chemical penetration and curing of the protective membranes.

Do you provide a guarantee certificate?

Yes, SPD provides a written 5 to 10-year warranty (depending on the system chosen) against leakage, backed by our rigorous quality-control process.
