Design Insights

Interior FAQs

Planning your dream home? Here's what you need to know about our design process.

What is your design-to-delivery timeline?

Typically, a full 2BHK/3BHK luxury interior project takes 60-90 days from the date of 3D design approval, depending on the complexity of customized woodwork.

Do you provide 3D visualizations?

Yes, we provide photorealistic 3D renders so you can see exactly how your home will look—including lighting, textures, and furniture—before we even touch the site.

How do you handle site management in occupied homes?

We use dust-management protocols and schedule heavy work during specific hours to minimize disturbance. However, for full renovations, we recommend temporary relocation for the best results.
