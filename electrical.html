Expert Answers

Electrical FAQs

Common questions homeowners in Thane ask about their electrical systems.

Do you provide AMC for residential societies?

Yes, we offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for premium residential complexes in Thane. This includes monthly safety audits, earthing checks, and emergency breakdown support.

How often should I check my home's earthing?

In high-rise buildings, we recommend a professional earthing test every 2 years or if you notice mild shocks from appliances. We use digital earth testers for precise measurements.

Are your electricians certified?

All SPD electricians are PWD license holders with a minimum of 8 years of experience in luxury residential projects. Safety is our non-negotiable priority.
