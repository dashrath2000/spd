Expert Answers

Renovation FAQs

Everything you need to know about remodeling your Thane home.

Do you handle TMC/Society permissions?

While the owner typically applies for official permissions, our architectural team provides all necessary drawings and structural stability certificates required by the Thane Municipal Corporation or your society office.

Can I renovate my home in phases?

Yes, we can plan the project in phases (e.g., Kitchen & Bathrooms first, then Living Areas). This helps manage budgets and allows you to occupy parts of the home during work.

Is waste management included?

Our turnkey package includes professional debris removal and eco-friendly disposal according to local regulations, ensuring your society premises remain clean.
